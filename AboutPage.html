ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

We are a bespoke bicycle shop in the centre of town that caters to the public as well as the professional cycling industry. Our team is made up of bike lovers with extensive experience in the cycling industry. Pop in and pay us a visit or browse our online store, we are ready to help with whatever you may need.

-


Mike Froome

Mike is about as real of a cyclist as they come. Born in Kenya, raised in South Africa and now residing in the UK. A cyclist by profession, he has raced on the world cycling tour for some of the most prestigious teams since he was a teenager, oh did we mention that he won the Tour De France FOUR times. We think its safe to say that he knows his way around a bike, he is at the helm of the shop making sure that everything happens just as its supposed to. Need some adive on bearings? ask Mike. Need some help choosing a bottom bracket? speak to Mike. He will always give you his best and honest opinion.


Simon Clarke

Simon is another lover of bicycles, born and raised in West Australia, he hopped on a bicycle from a young age and his talents were quickly realised. He has ridden his bicycle all around the world, through many conditions and circumstances. Lets just say that he has broken a lot of gear, so that you dont have to, he knows what works and what doesn't. Pop in for a coffee and a chat with Simon and he will guide you to what will best suit you and your needs.
